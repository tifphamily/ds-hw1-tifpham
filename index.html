About Me

Born in 1996, Tiffany was raised on Mars in a family of five. On Mars, Tiffany enjoyed activites such as dancing, playing piano, and trying new food. Upon graduation from Mars State High School, she attended Indiana University's Kelley School of Business where she majored in Accounting and Information Systems. After working several accounting internships, Tiffany realized she was more passionate in purusing a career in technology consulting. Because of this, she enrolled in the MS in Information Systems program at IU to gain more techical background relating to her field of work.
